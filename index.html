Rules and Regulations

  • No smoking inside the hostel premises.
  • No loud noise after 10:00 PM.
  • Guests are not allowed to stay overnight.
  • Mess food timings should be strictly followed.
  • Attendance is mandatory for hostel meetings.
  • Do not tamper with hostel property.
  • Internet usage limits are enforced.
  • In case of emergencies, contact the warden.
  • Cleanliness must be maintained in rooms.
  • No unauthorized parties or gatherings.
